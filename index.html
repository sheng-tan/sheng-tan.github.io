About

Sheng Tan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Trinity University. Prior to Trinity, he received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science at Florida State University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Jie Yang.

His research interests span Mobile Computering, Cybersecurity, Human Computer Interaction and Computer Science Education. He published papers in premier venues such as ACM CHI, ACM CCS, ACM MobiHoc, IEEE INFOCOM and ACM Ubicomp. His current projects include mobile safety system for distracted driver/pedestrian and mobile sensing for human computer interaction applications.
